When you pick up a slice, your team's score will be increased by 1. The game ends and resets when one team has reached the score threshold.

The aim of the game to go around the map and collect the pizza slices that randomly spawn.

When you first launch the game, you will spawn in a random location. Use WASD or the on-screen buttons to move.
